Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 8, 2026

Novra ("we", "us", or "our") is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your data when you use the Novra app.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly, including:

  • Account information (name, email address)
  • Health and fitness data (workouts, nutrition logs, body metrics)
  • Photos submitted for AI body analysis
  • Device information and usage analytics

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your data to:

  • Provide and personalize the Novra experience
  • Generate AI-powered workout and meal plans
  • Analyze your progress and deliver insights
  • Improve our features and app performance
  • Communicate with you about your account and updates

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We may share data with trusted third-party service providers (e.g., analytics, cloud infrastructure) solely to operate the app. These partners are bound by confidentiality obligations and may not use your data for their own purposes.

4. Health Data

Health and body-related data is treated with the utmost care. Photos submitted for AI analysis are processed securely and are not used for any purpose other than providing your analysis. You can delete your data at any time from within the app.

5. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active, or as needed to provide services. You may request deletion at any time by emailing support@novra.fit.

6. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights to access, correct, or delete your personal data. Contact us at support@novra.fit to exercise these rights.

7. Contact Us

For privacy-related questions, contact us at support@novra.fit.
